Towaco, NJ Insights: Population, Influence, and Service Areas

Towaco /təˈwɑː.koʊ/ is an unincorporated community and census-designated place (CDP) located within Montville Township in Morris County, in the U.S. state of New Jersey. The area is served as United States Postal Service ZIP Code 07082. Its name reflects the Native American history in the area, which identified the locale as TaWagh, meaning 'hill', a reference to the terrain of Towaco.
